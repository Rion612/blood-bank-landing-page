About us

We are a non‑profit charity organization that connects voluntary donors with patients in need, helping people donate blood free of cost.

Founded as Lifeline, we’re known in regional areas of Bangladesh as রক্তবন্ধু (Rokto Bondhu). Our community currently includes about 35 members across different blood groups — and you can join too.

Eligibility

  • Age 18–65 years

  • Weight ≥ 50 kg (110 lb)

  • Hemoglobin within normal range

  • No donation in the last 56 days

  • Free from cold, flu, or infections

  • Not pregnant or recently delivered

  • No recent tattoos/piercings (last 6 months)

  • Not under antibiotic treatment

  • Well hydrated and rested

  • Bring photo ID

These are general guidelines. Final eligibility is determined by on-site medical staff.

Frequently asked questions

How often can I donate?

Whole blood: every 56 days. Platelets and plasma may have different intervals.

Is it safe?

Yes. We use sterile, single-use equipment and trained staff for every donation.

What should I do before donating?

Eat a healthy meal, drink water, and bring a photo ID. Avoid strenuous exercise right after donating.
